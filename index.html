More about the Ocean Credit Card

The Ocean Credit Card is issued by Capital One (Europe) and has an
APR from 24.9% APR (variable) with no annual fee. There's an initial
credit limit of £200 to £8,000 and the amount offered varies by
customer depending on their credit history and financial situation.
Ocean Finance is a trading name of Intelligent Lending Limited.
